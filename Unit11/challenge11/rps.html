Game Rules!

Pick between Rock, Paper, Scissors. Then, the computer will randomly choose rock, paper, or scissors as well. Scissors beat Paper, Paper beats Rock, and Rock beats Scissors. Anything else results in a "tie". You have five rounds to play. Each win gives +1 points, lose reduces -1 points, and no points for a tie.
